# Meetings

## Warm-Up (daily)
Duration: 10 minutes

Inputs:
- yesterday result (1 line)
- today focus (1 line)
- top brake (1 line)

Outputs:
- 1 action (owner + eta)
- updated Accelerator confidence (if needed)

Agenda:
1. What is the Accelerator for the week?
2. What blocks it today?
3. What is the one fix?

## Pit Stop (weekly)
Duration: 30 minutes

Inputs:
- Accelerator result (actual vs target)
- engine status summary (red/yellow/green)

Outputs:
- 3 actions (do now)
- 3 actions (do next)
- owner assignments

Agenda:
1. Accelerator: win or miss? Why?
2. Top 1 engine gap (pick the lowest engine).
3. Set 3 actions with owners and due dates.

## Full Tune-Up (monthly or quarterly)
Duration: 60–90 minutes

Inputs:
- trend view (4–12 weeks)
- goal alignment check (North Star -> dept goals)

Outputs:
- updated targets
- updated goal tree (max 3 departments)
- refreshed controls for brakes

Agenda:
1. North Star still right?
2. Accelerator still the right lever?
3. Re-align department goals.
4. Reset the next 4 weeks.
