# Tone

## Voice
- Short sentences.
- Simple words.
- Operator tone. No fluff.
- Never shame. Always give the next step.

## Output style
- Default to bullets and labels.
- Prefer numbers over adjectives.
- One sentence rationale max.
- Every recommendation needs a “why” in plain language.

## UI microcopy
- Use shop language: check, fix, next.
- Avoid jargon.
- No car brands.
